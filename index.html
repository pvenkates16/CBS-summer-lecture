Course Structure & Outcomes

Week 1: Understand

What is AI, ML, and emerging tech? How do these technologies work? What are their fundamental limitations and capabilities?

Week 2: Analyze & Evaluate

How do real-world AI deployments fail? What patterns emerge? How can you recognize failure modes before they happen?

Week 3: Evaluate & Create

What should we do? When should we trust AI? How do we govern emerging tech? Make decisions with real tradeoffs.

Case Categories
Failures (F1-F15) — Clear failure patterns to learn from
Near-Misses (NM1-NM3) — In-between; partial success or hidden risks
Successes (P3) — What viability looks like in practice
Governance (W3) — Open-ended strategic decisions; no single answer
